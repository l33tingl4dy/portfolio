What is St. Jude Children's Research Hospital?

St. Jude is a hospital near my hometown in Memphis, TN. They treat children with various cancers and never charge families for any of it.

St. Jude is leading the way the world understands, treats and defeats childhood cancer and other life-threatening diseases. And families never receive a bill from St. Jude for treatment, travel, housing or food — because all a family should worry about is helping their child live. St. Jude FAQ

What do donations pay for?

  • $10 can provide a new toy for hospital play areas
  • $25 can provide a No More Chemo Party for St. Jude patients and their families, or it can provide rehabilitation weights for a patient
  • $50 can provide art supplies to St. Jude patients
  • $100 can provide red wagons to patients: the preferred mode of transportation for younger patients. $100 could also provide a pair of pediatric crutches
  • $250 can provide a meal card for a St. Jude Family for one week
  • $500 can provide a share of bone marrow treatment
  • $1000 can cover 2/3 of the cost of one day of chemotherapy
  • Also, masks, gowns, and other supplies that are needed daily!
